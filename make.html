Abacha is a product of cassava tubers. When the processing of it is done from scratch, harvested cassava is obtained, peeled, washed, cooked and sliced into desired sizes or shapes. It is soaked in a bowl, bucket, etc., for a day. It could prepared instantly as abacha or sun-dried for future use. shredded dried cassava first needs to be softened by soaking in boiled water and covered up for few minutes.

The decision to add or not to add various condiments/additional ingredients is also partly dependent on whether it is intended to be consumed as a light, cold snack or more-or-less stand-alone (or at least in-between) meal. Abacha can serve both purposes.
